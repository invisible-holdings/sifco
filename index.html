Who We Are

A Foundation Built on
Purpose & Evidence

"Digital safety is not a luxury — it is a fundamental right of every child and community."

SIFCO Foundation — the Safer Initiative for Communities Organisation — is a registered non-profit based in Lesotho, working across sub-Saharan Africa to protect children from digital harm, bridge technological divides, and create pathways to economic opportunity through digital skills.

We take a systems approach: combining policy advocacy, community education, vocational training, and technological innovation to create lasting, measurable change. Our programs reach youth in rehabilitation centers, rural communities, and urban schools — ensuring that no child is left behind in the digital age.

What We Do

Our Strategic Objectives

Grounded in the UN Sustainable Development Goals and driven by community need, our eight program areas address the most urgent challenges facing youth and communities in the digital age.

01 — PROTECTION
🛡️

Child & Youth Protection

Protecting children and youth from violence, exploitation, and digital abuse through community-driven safeguards, policy advocacy, and targeted education programs.

02 — CLIMATE
🌱

Climate-Smart Digital Initiatives

Deploying AI-powered climate vulnerability tools and social change programs to build a climate-aware generation equipped to respond to environmental challenges.

03 — ACCESS
🌐

Bridging the Digital Divide

Advocating for secure, affordable, and community-trusted internet tools — ensuring equitable access to digital infrastructure across underserved regions.

04 — SAFETY
⚠️

Anti-Trafficking Awareness

Educating communities and youth on the intersection of technology and human trafficking — building awareness and resilience against exploitation online.

05 — SKILLS
🎓

Vocational & Digital Skills

Delivering certified vocational and digital training programs that equip youth with practical, market-relevant capabilities for sustained economic participation.

06 — INNOVATION
🚀

Digital Inclusion & Innovation

Catalyzing youth-led innovation, entrepreneurship, and employment through digital platforms, incubation support, and ecosystem partnerships.

07 — LITERACY
📚

Digital Literacy & E-Learning

Delivering digital literacy and e-learning programs in rehabilitation centers, detention facilities, and Survivor Care institutions — reaching the most vulnerable.

08 — ECONOMY
💼

Digital Livelihoods & Marketplace

Connecting upskilled youth to digital marketplaces and remote work opportunities — transforming newly acquired skills into sustainable livelihoods.

About the Foundation

Who We Are &
Why We Exist

SIFCO Foundation was established to address a critical gap: as digital technologies reshape African societies, too many children and communities are being left behind — or worse, placed at greater risk.

Our Story

From Lesotho to the World

Founded in Maseru, Lesotho, SIFCO Foundation emerged from direct observation of the harms that unregulated digital access was causing in vulnerable communities — from children exposed to exploitation online, to youth unable to access the economic opportunities the internet offered.

Our founders recognized that the solution required more than awareness campaigns. It required a sustained, multi-sectoral approach combining education, policy reform, community mobilization, and skills development — backed by data and delivered with cultural sensitivity.

Today, SIFCO operates across six African countries, with partnerships spanning government ministries, international development organizations, the private sector, and civil society. We measure our success not by activities, but by outcomes: safer children, employed youth, and more resilient communities.

"We believe that every child, regardless of geography or circumstance, deserves to be safe, connected, and equipped for the future."

The SIFCO Training Room is not a self-paced course platform. It is a live, virtual workshop space where registered participants join scheduled sessions with expert facilitators and guest speakers. Sessions are interactive, community-focused, and free of charge for qualifying participants.
